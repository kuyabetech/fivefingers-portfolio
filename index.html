About Five Fingers

At Five Fingers, we serve up fast food with a side of love! Located at Abdullahi Fodio University of Science and Technology, Aliero, Kebbi State, our passion for fresh ingredients, quick service, and a student-friendly vibe makes us the go-to spot for delicious meals. From spicy Indomie and Egg to flavorful Shawarma, every dish is crafted to satisfy your cravings. Join us for a fun dining experience!
